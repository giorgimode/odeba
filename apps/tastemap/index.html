Features

🗺️
Your world fills in as you eat
An interactive world map colors every country you've eaten from. Log a Georgian Khachapuri and Georgia lights up. Try Pho and Vietnam joins the map. Watch your culinary footprint grow.
🌍
🍜
Massive dish catalog
Search through 7,000+ real dishes sourced from Wikidata, complete with descriptions, origin stories, native names, and ingredients.
7,085 dishes · 195 countries
🏆
Earn achievements
Unlock tiered badges from Bronze to Diamond as you explore. Dish Collector, Globetrotter, Continent Conqueror — your culinary journey becomes a game.
📍
Remember every meal
Log where you tried a dish — restaurant name, date, photos, and a rating for that specific spot. Try the same dish in Tokyo and Rome, and track them separately.
❤️
Food bucket list
Add dishes and entire countries to your wishlist. Plan your next culinary adventure before you even book the flight.
🌐
Native names in 50 languages
See dishes in their original script — 炸酱面, おでん, 피자. Search by native name too, so you can find exactly what you saw on that menu.
📸
Photos & memories
Attach photos to dishes and visits. Set a hero shot that replaces the catalog image with your own memory.
